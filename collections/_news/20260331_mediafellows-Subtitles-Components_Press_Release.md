      <p>
        <strong><big>Automated Subtitle Generation</big></strong>         
      </p>
       <p>
        A cherry-picked selection of the most powerful AI/ML services built into MediaStore offers multilingual <strong>subtitle creation in minutes</strong> — enabling fast global marketing decisions. Built for internal review, buyers can assess the market viability of content for any territory before committing to a full localization suite, without relying on external vendors or wasting resources on rejected content. Simply select the original audio track (now available as a component within MediaStore's new modular asset system) to instantly transcribe it, generate a subtitle in the original language, and translate it into multiple target languages.
      </p>
      <p>
	   Additionally, a full localization suite of professionally created subtitles, complete with styling, can be ordered from specialist vendors such as Pixelogic, directly within MediaStore via the <strong>Localization Order Management</strong> feature. While AI-generated subtitles offer speed and accessibility for internal review decisions, professional localization remains the gold standard for consumer-facing content — offering full styling, language scope, and quality assurance that automated services cannot yet match.
          </p>
          <p>
       <strong>A leading global streaming platform</strong> operating across 240 countries and territories has already adopted MediaStore’s subtitle generation to accelerate acquisition decisions — reducing upload-to-approval from days and weeks to minutes and hours.
        </p>

     <p>
   <strong><big>Flexible Modular Video Components</big></strong>   
      </p>
      <p>
        Conceived to reduce clutter and enhance the flexibility of video management and playout, MediaStore now supports audio tracks, subtitles, and pre-rolls as independent video components within a single video asset. Tracks can be switched directly in the video player, <strong>enhancing accessibility</strong> options across languages, audio formats, and subtitle types — including SDH (Subtitles for the Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing).
      </p>
      <p>
		Forced narratives ensure a seamless viewing experience by automatically displaying subtitle tracks when on-screen dialogue switches to a different language, even if subtitles are otherwise disabled in the player. <strong>Pre-rolls</strong> (video snippets played before the full screener or episodic) can also be used for ads, copyright notices, teaser trailers, or even recaps — assigned and applied flexibly to multiple videos as needed.
      </p>

      <p>
        <strong>One of the world's most recognized technology companies</strong> and streaming platforms recently eliminated a catalog-wide manual re-edit during a global logo rebrand using MediaStore's pre-roll functionality. Previously, idents were stitched directly in each video, which meant every adjustment required a full re-edit across hundreds of titles. By attaching the logo-branded ident as a pre-roll, the file was replaced just once inside the shell asset and automatically updated across their entire catalog, with <strong>zero manual work</strong>.
      </p>
      <p>
		By adding features only previously seen in B2C services, such as forced narratives and seamless switching between subtitle and audio tracks, MediaStore significantly improved the platform's end-user experience. Following migration, the streaming platform reported a substantial reduction in support requests, with several users reaching out unprompted to note it was not only a significant improvement on their previous platform, but a better experience than many competitors.
