<div class="job-details mb-40">

<p>
<strong>mediafellows</strong> is a leading software provider for the media and entertainment industry. Headquartered in Berlin, we provide cloud-based content management, marketing, and distribution solutions (SaaS) for broadcasters, studios, and distributors worldwide. Our tools are used to screen, sell, and securely manage high-value media assets.
</p>
<p>
We are looking for an <strong>experienced software engineer</strong> to architect, develop, and maintain our suite of native video applications. You will be responsible for ensuring a seamless video viewing experience across <strong>Android Mobile, Amazon Fire TV, and Roku</strong> devices. You will bridge the gap between our complex backend media services and the end-user experience, ensuring high fidelity, security, and usability.
</p>

<hr>

<h5>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</h5>

<p>
<strong>Native Application Development</strong>
<ul>
  <li>Develop and maintain the mediafellows native applications for <strong>Android</strong> (Mobiles and Tablets), <strong>Fire TV</strong> (Android TV OS), and <strong>Roku</strong>.</li>
  <li>Write clean, maintainable code using <strong>Kotlin</strong> for Android/Fire TV and <strong>BrightScript/SceneGraph</strong> for Roku.</li>
</ul>
</p>

<p>
<strong>UI/UX Implementation</strong>
<ul>
  <li>Implement flexible, responsive screen layouts that adapt seamlessly between touch interfaces (mobile) and D-pad navigation (TV/10-foot UI).</li>
  <li>Ensure strict adherence to platform-specific Human Interface Guidelines (Google Material Design for TV, Roku Design Guidelines).</li>
</ul>
</p>

<p>
<strong>Video Streaming & Security</strong>
<ul>
  <li>Integrate <strong>DASH</strong> for high-quality adaptive bitrate streaming.</li>
  <li>Implement DRM and other <strong>Video Streaming Security</strong> solutions to protect our clients’ valuable video content.</li>
  <li>Engineer robust <strong>file and metadata download</strong> features to support offline viewing modes with secure local storage encryption.</li>
</ul>
</p>

<p>
<strong>Backend Integration</strong>
<ul>
  <li>Connect applications to our MediaStore backend microservices using RESTful APIs.</li>
  <li>Implement <strong>JSON Schema</strong> validation to ensure strict data contracts and prevent app crashes from backend data structure changes.</li>
</ul>
</p>

<p>
<strong>Lifecycle & Support</strong>
<ul>
  <li>Manage the full app lifecycle, including submission to Google Play, Amazon Appstore, and the Roku Channel Store.</li>
  <li>Provide <strong>ongoing maintenance</strong>, bug fixing, and performance monitoring for deployed apps.</li>
</ul>
</p>

<hr>

<h5>TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS</h5>
<p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Experience</strong> 5+ years in mobile development, with 2+ years in TV platforms (Android TV or Roku).</li>
  <li><strong>Android/Fire TV Stack</strong> Expert knowledge of Kotlin, Android SDK, Android Jetpack, and the Leanback Library for TV UIs.</li>
  <li><strong>Roku Stack</strong> Proficiency in BrightScript and the SceneGraph XML framework. Experience with Roku native components (Lists, Grids, Video nodes).</li>
  <li><strong>Video Streaming</strong> Deep understanding of video players, such as ExoPlayer, for custom playback controls and DASH handling, as well as MPEG-DASH manifests (.mpd), HLS, and DRM concepts.</li>
  <li><strong>Data & Networking</strong> Strong experience with Retrofit/OkHttp (Android) and Roku Network tasks. Ability to write and validate JSON Schemas.</li>
</ul>
</p>

<hr>

<h5>NICE TO HAVE</h5>
<p>
<ul>
  <li>Fluent in the written and spoken English language.</li>
  <li>Experience with automated UI testing (Espresso, Appium, or Roku WebDriver).</li>
  <li>Background in B2B media or VOD (Video on Demand) sectors.</li>
  <li>Knowledge of iOS/tvOS development (Swift) is a major plus.</li>
  <li>Hands-on experience in applying Web Content Accessibility Guidelines principles and success criteria to mobile and TV applications.</li>
</ul>
</p>
