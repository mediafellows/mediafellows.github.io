<h2>Your Opportunity</h2>
<p>
  <strong>mediafellows</strong> is a leading software provider for the media and entertainment industry. Headquartered in Berlin, we provide cloud-based content management, marketing, and distribution solutions (SaaS) for broadcasters, studios, and distributors worldwide. Our tools are used to screen, sell, and securely manage high-value media assets.
</p>
<p>
  We are seeking a highly experienced <strong>Senior DevOps Engineer</strong> to help provision, secure, automate, and operate the AWS-based infrastructure supporting our <strong>MediaStore</strong> platform and related services. A strong focus of this role is infrastructure security, operational reliability, and automation.
</p>
<p>
  This is a unique position to work at the forefront of the transformation of the media industry from on-prem to cloud-based workflows. We look for a new colleague with skill, passion, humor, and a decent sense of responsibility who help us raise our solution to the next level with state-of-the-art web technologies.
</p>

<p>
<h2>Your Responsibilities</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Provision, maintain, and secure AWS-based production infrastructure</li>
  <li>Design and operate highly available, fault-tolerant cloud environments</li>
  <li>Implement and improve CI/CD pipelines and automated deployment workflows</li>
  <li>Collaborate with developers to bring new services and features into production</li>
  <li>Help establish and spread DevOps practices across engineering teams</li>
  <li>Support and troubleshoot large-scale production environments</li>
  <li>Implement monitoring, alerting, logging, and observability solutions</li>
  <li>Improve infrastructure security, hardening, and operational resilience</li>
  <li>Support infrastructure automation and Infrastructure-as-Code initiatives</li>
  <li>Ensure reliable environments for media processing and streaming workloads</li>
</ul>
</p>

<p>
<h2>Required Qualifications</h2>
<ul>
  <li>5+ years of hands-on experience in provisioning and operating production multi-service infrastructures in the cloud</li>
  <li>Strong Linux / Unix administration skills</li>
  <li>Deep knowledge of AWS infrastructure and services</li>
  <li>Experience with REST APIs, SOAP/XML, JSON, YAML</li>
  <li>Strong understanding of RDBMS and NoSQL databases</li>
  <li>Experience with Git and modern version control workflows</li>
  <li>Experience designing and operating CI/CD systems (Jenkins, GitHub Actions, etc.)</li>
  <li>Experience with configuration management (Ansible, Puppet, etc.)</li>
  <li>Experience with Docker and containerized environments</li>
  <li>Familiarity with orchestration technologies (Kubernetes, Docker Swarm, etc.)</li>
  <li>Strong understanding of networking fundamentals, firewalls, load balancing, and infrastructure security</li>
  <li>Experience with monitoring and observability tooling (NewRelic, Grafana, etc.)</li>
  <li>Experience in troubleshooting production incidents and large-scale operational issues</li>
  <li>Ability to work independently with minimal supervision</li>
</ul>
</p>

<p>
<h2>Nice to Have</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Security-focused DevOps background</li>
  <li>Experience in media delivery or streaming platforms</li>
  <li>Familiarity with security hardening and infrastructure security best practices</li>
  <li>Experience with security audits (TPN, ISO 27001, etc.)</li>
</ul>
</p>

<h2>What we Offer</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Competitive salary package with perks and/or bonus options</li>
  <li>Flexible working hours and remote work options</li>
  <li>A key role in an international team of industry veterans</li>
  <li>The opportunity to work with high-profile content from major global studios</li>
</ul>
</p>
